Work Samples

See the deliverables before you engage.

Two complete demonstration packages — workbook plus methodology memorandum — built on wholly fictional, synthetically generated data, watermarked as demonstrations on every page.

S1

Unpaid Overtime Exposure Analysis

A fictional proposed class proceeding: 43,858 punch-clock rows across five years, reconciled against payroll. Deliverables include a data-quality log, weekly overtime calculations under BC employment standards rules, claim-favourable and defence-favourable scenarios, limitation-period sensitivity, and a consulting memorandum with numbered assumptions.

S2

Fidelity Claim Data Support

A fictional employee-dishonesty claim: 847 bank payments matched to 713 invoices, payments flagged under criteria specified by a retaining forensic accountant, totals by criterion and year, and a documentary event chronology with source references — reported with no findings and no opinions.

Claims data support — sample

What the samples show

  • Raw records preserved as received, defects intact, on their own tabs
  • A cleaning log itemizing every defect type, its detection rule, and its resolution rule
  • Formula-driven calculations — every figure in the memo traces to a cell
  • Contested judgment calls expressed as scenarios, not buried in a single number
  • Limitations stated plainly, including what the analysis does not do
