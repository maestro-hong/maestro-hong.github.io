Litigation Data Support

For counsel and retained experts on civil matters: cleaning and reconciling high-volume records, applying calculation rules as instructed, building timelines and schedules, and producing exhibit-ready tables with a documented methodology memo.

Quoted per matter — hourly

02

Claims & Incident Data Support

For forensic accountants, licensed adjusters, and claims teams: claim chronologies with source references, loss summaries totalled under specified criteria, incident-record organization, and missing-document inventories on large files.

Quoted per matter — hourly

03

Data Triage

A fixed-scope starting point. Send the document set; receive a short written memo describing what it contains, its condition and defects, and what analysis it will and will not support — before anyone commits to a larger scope.

$1,500 — fixed fee, one week

How engagements work

  • Under direction. A retaining professional — counsel, a forensic accountant, an adjuster, or a retained expert — specifies the rules, criteria, and scope. We apply them mechanically and document everything.
  • Client-provided records only. We analyze what is provided to us. We do not gather information about people or organizations.
  • Built for a second reviewer. Deliverables include a methodology memo with itemized assumptions, a data-quality log, and figures that trace to formula-driven cells.
  • Confidential and conflict-checked. Every matter is screened against a conflicts log before acceptance.

Scope

Unio Analytics provides data and reporting support only. It does not conduct investigations, adjust or assess claims, provide security consulting, or offer expert opinions on fraud, coverage, liability, or damages. Those functions belong to the licensed and qualified professionals we support.
